# Frequently Asked Questions

A collection of FAQs our customers find it helpful.

---

### Where can I purchase XDAQ AIO?

1. Visit the [XDAQ AIO](https://www.kontex.io/xdaq) webpage.
2. Click `Add to Wishlist`.
3. Go to `Wishlist` at the top of the website and select `Quote Wishlist`.
4. Fill in the required information and click `Submit Quote`.

---

### No Preview After Initial Setup?

Re-select the same `Quality` / `Codec` once.

If the issue persists, repeat the [Launch Sequence](user-manual.md#launch-sequence).

---

### Lost Connection?

Confirm all cables are secure, then wait for the [XDAQ status](user-manual.md#3-xdaq-status) to update (approx. 6 seconds).

If the issue persists, repeat the [Launch Sequence](user-manual.md#launch-sequence).

---

### Preview Frozen After a Mid-Demo Change?

Repeat the [Launch Sequence](user-manual.md#launch-sequence).

---

### How can I ensure video synchronization between USB cameras and Headstage signals?

We provide detailed documentation on how we measure [camera latency](camera-latency.md) and [metadata processing](metadata-processing.md) the camera frame with headstage signals acquire by [XDAQ-RHX](https://github.com/kontex-neuro/Intan-RHX).

/// note | Note
Support for [Open Ephys GUI](https://open-ephys.org/gui) is in development (coming soon).
///

---

### Why aren't my cameras working?

If you're experiencing issues with USB cameras, please refer to our [USB cameras](usb-cameras.md) documentation for troubleshooting steps and compatibility information.
